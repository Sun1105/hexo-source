<div class="title">业务逻辑 vs 技术实现 对照图</div>
<div class="sub">以电商系统「订单下单流程」为例</div>

<div class="table">

<div></div>
<div class="header h-left">业务逻辑（做什么 / 为什么）</div>
<div class="header h-mid">对应关系</div>
<div class="header h-right">技术实现（怎么做）</div>

<!-- Step 1 -->
<div class="cell step">1<br>创建请求</div>
<div class="cell biz">
<div class="section-title">业务规则</div>
用户必须提交订单信息（商品、数量、地址）
</div>
<div class="cell rel">HTTP请求</div>
<div class="cell tech">
POST /order/create<br>
Controller接收参数 DTO
</div>

<!-- Step 2 -->
<div class="cell step">2<br>校验数据</div>
<div class="cell biz">
<div class="section-title">业务规则</div>
• 商品必须存在<br>
• 数量 > 0<br>
• 地址不能为空
</div>
<div class="cell rel">参数校验</div>
<div class="cell tech">
if(product == null)<br>
if(count <= 0)<br>
if(address == null)
</div>

<!-- Step 3 -->
<div class="cell step">3<br>库存校验</div>
<div class="cell biz">
<div class="section-title">业务规则</div>
库存必须充足，否则不能下单
</div>
<div class="cell rel">业务判断</div>
<div class="cell tech">
select stock from db<br>
if(stock < count) throw error
</div>

<!-- Step 4 -->
<div class="cell step">4<br>金额计算</div>
<div class="cell biz">
<div class="section-title">业务规则</div>
总金额 = 商品金额 + 运费 - 优惠
</div>
<div class="cell rel">计算逻辑</div>
<div class="cell tech">
total = price * count<br>
+ shippingFee<br>
- discount
</div>

<!-- Step 5 -->
<div class="cell step">5<br>生成订单</div>
<div class="cell biz">
<div class="section-title">业务规则</div>
必须生成唯一订单号并保存
</div>
<div class="cell rel">持久化</div>
<div class="cell tech">
insert into order<br>
generate orderId
</div>

<!-- Step 6 -->
<div class="cell step">6<br>扣减库存</div>
<div class="cell biz">
<div class="section-title">业务规则</div>
下单成功必须扣减库存
</div>
<div class="cell rel">事务处理</div>
<div class="cell tech">
update stock set stock = stock - count
</div>

<!-- Step 7 -->
<div class="cell step">7<br>返回结果</div>
<div class="cell biz">
<div class="section-title">业务规则</div>
返回订单信息和状态
</div>
<div class="cell rel">响应封装</div>
<div class="cell tech">
return ResponseEntity(orderId, success)
</div>

</div>

<div class="footer">
<b>总结：</b><br><br>
✔ 业务逻辑：决定“系统应该怎么运作”（规则）<br>
✔ 技术实现：决定“系统如何实现这些规则”（代码）<br><br>

<b>关键理解：</b><br>
👉 业务逻辑可以脱离代码存在（写在设计书里）<br>
👉 技术实现只是业务逻辑的一种表达方式
</div>
